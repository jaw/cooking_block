How much does it cost?

Three monthly subscriptions to choose from

Platinum

The platinum subscription is for 60 hours or more* of kitchen usage per month.

  • Priority scheduling
  • Permanent cold storage
  • Wi-Fi
  • Utilities included
  • Schedule during premium hours 7AM - 7PM
  • $20.00/hour

*Requires $500.00 security deposit

Gold

The gold subscription is for 40 to 60 hours* of kitchen usage per month.

  • Priority scheduling
  • Permanent cold storage
  • Wi-Fi
  • Utilities included
  • Schedule during premium hours 7AM - 7PM
  • $25.00/hour

*Requires $500.00 security deposit

Silver

The silver subscription is for 20 to 40 hours* of kitchen usage per month.

  • Priority scheduling
  • Permanent cold storage for additional $75.00/month
  • Wi-Fi
  • Utilities included
  • Schedule during premium hours 7AM - 7PM
  • $25.00/hour

*Requires $500.00 security deposit

After hours subscriptions

Schedule your Platinum, Gold or Silver subscription during after hours (7PM - 7AM) and receive 20% off!

Custom subscriptions

A 6 month agreement allows you to build your own custom subscription tailored to your specific needs with the benefits included in any of the above subscriptions based on the number of hours in the agreement.
