Commercial kitchen rental

Every business needs a home. If you are searching for a licensed commercial kitchen to operate from you have come to the right place. We have designed four separate kitchens equipped with everything you need.

We provide our clients with a host of support services. You have the idea to produce an amazing food product, but how are you going to package, market and distribute that product? How do you license your business or product to be in compliance with federal, state and local laws? Cooking Block is here to back you up.

Cooking classes and Culinary education

We offer cooking classes on every subject, from learning the basics to advanced skill building. Food should be fun and so should cooking classes! Food brings people together and so do our classes!
