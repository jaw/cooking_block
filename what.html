Commercial kitchen rental

Every business needs a home. Cooking Block was built to be that home! Join our community of food entrepreneurs and operate your food business from Cooking Block.

We proudly offer three fully equipped, Health Department approved commercial kitchens. With our business model each booking you make allows you to have an entire kitchen to yourself with a minimum of 18 linear feet of prep tables space in each kitchen.

Look over the robust features of our facility:

  • 24/7/365 availability.
  • Self-guided booking from our website.
  • Cooklines include double-stack convection oven, flattop griddle, range, charbroiler, stock pot range, and jet wok burner.
  • Dry and cold storage available.
  • 2 compartment prep sinks.
  • Commercial dish machine washer and 3 compartment sink.
